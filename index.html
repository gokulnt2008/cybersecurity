What Is Online Safety ?

Online safety refers to be in a very safe position while surfing on the internet. Online safety can be related with the well being of one's personal privacy. We must be aware on certain circumstances on how to be safe on internet and how to overcome an uneasy situation. We should always be aware on certain circumstances and prevent ourselves from being spammed in the internet

Your Details

Never Reveal Personal Information

We must be careful while revealing our personal information in many places. We must be very careful in giving out our personal information to unknown people as this may lead to severe demolish someone’s privacy.

Precautions

• Do not give away any of your personal details to any unknown applications
• Do not give any sensitive information (pass codes, phone numbers, address, contact information etc) to application
• When you see that the content asked in a website are too personal, log off the site.

Do Not Talk To Unknown Strangers Across the internet

No matter whether you are in a game or in a social media site, there is mostly an option to interact with other users in the globe. However there are many privacy issues related in sharing over private information and here are the precautions taken

Precautions

• Hackers all around the world will be logged in to any website. They also might try to steal your personal information, but we must be aware and not give our personal information to the hackers.
• While in social media’s, when someone asks for your details (no matter who) just log off and don’t reply anything. The logic is to be protected while surfing the net.

Do not install unwanted and hazardous looking contents.

There are millions and billions of downloadable stuff on the internet, but not everything is safe out there. There are many contents that are not that safe to use or contain malware, which can steal the privacy of the clients. The most important concept in the internet is to prevent unknown installations. This is very important because the downloaded content may have the ability to read the information and send it to the hacker.

Precautions

• Never download any unknown software from the internet as this may lead to loss of privacy
• Always keep a good antivirus ready if you really want to install something.
• Always keep the firewall always activated

Follow The Age restrictions and privacy policy of a website
