Follow The Age restrictions and privacy policy of a website


Many websites have standard age restrictions along with a privacy policy. This is very important and has to be followed by the clients using the webpage. The age restrictions is also very important to filter the age group and the content accordingly

Precautions

• There can be content that cannot be children-friendly on a website. Thus the policy of age restrictions is maintained. And it is very important for following it.
• The privacy policy must be read thoroughly before using the application. This is to make sure that there are no spam or non-friendly actions that can be taken in the website.
• There are many parental control options on the net for protecting the kids from harmful content on the net. Namely

o Qustodio
o OpenDns
o Kasperky safe kids

Example on the problem

Imagine you installed an EXE installer of a social media site and downloaded it without reading the privacy policy and have entered your phone number. The nest day you get numerous messages from unknown people and when you browsed the new social media app you found that the entire users phone numbers where present there and they where the one who were texting you all night. Now you went to read the privacy policy and found that your phone number will be known to everybody using the app. This is a small problem that can be fixed but what if there were some big issues caused because of not reading the privacy policy of a website? So no matter how big a privacy policy looks just read it thoroughly.
