We must be careful while revealing our personal information in many places. We must be very careful in giving out our personal information to unknown people as this may lead to a severe demolishment someone’s privacy.

Precautions

• Do not give away any of your personal details to any unknown applications
• Do not give any sensitive information (pass codes, phone numbers, address, contact information etc) to application
• When you see that the content asked in a website are too personal, log off the site.

Why hackers need it?

• Your personal information can be traded by hackers and also can be published in the internet.
• Hackers can also trade your contact information to companies that advertise by calling or messaging to others.

Problems Caused

• Your phone numbers or contact information can be spammed or important account details can be read and a unknown transaction can also take place.
• Your private data like photos can be read and posted on the internet.
• Another huge problem is when the hacker posts unwanted contents by logging in your account.
